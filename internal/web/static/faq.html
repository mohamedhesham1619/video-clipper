Frequently Asked Questions

How does VideoClipper work?
VideoClipper is a free online tool that allows you to create custom video clips from various platforms. Simply paste a video URL, set your start and end times, choose your preferred quality, and download your clip instantly.
Which video platforms are supported?
We support over 1000+ video sites including YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, Vimeo, Reddit, and many more. Check our Supported Sites page for the complete list.
Is VideoClipper free to use?
Yes! VideoClipper is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium features. All functionality is available to everyone at no cost.
What video qualities are available?
We support multiple quality options including 360p, 480p, 720p, and 1080p. The available qualities depend on the original video's resolution and the platform's restrictions.
How long can my clips be?
You can create clips of any length, from a few seconds to several minutes. However, very long clips may take longer to process and download.
Is it legal to use VideoClipper?
VideoClipper is a tool for creating clips from publicly available content. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to use and modify any content they process. Please respect copyright laws and platform terms of service.
Do you store my videos?
No, we do not store your videos permanently. Videos are processed temporarily to create your clips and are automatically deleted after processing is complete.
What if my clip fails to process?
If your clip fails to process, it could be due to platform restrictions, video availability, or technical issues. Try again with a different time range or contact us for support.
Can I use VideoClipper on mobile devices?
Yes! VideoClipper is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The interface automatically adjusts to provide the best experience on your screen size.
How can I contact support?
You can contact us through our Contact page or use the floating contact button on any page. We typically respond within 24 hours.
