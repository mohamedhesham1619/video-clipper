Common Questions

What is VideoClipper?

VideoClipper is a free online tool that lets you extract and download a specific part or clip from a video â€” no installation or login required.

Is VideoClipper free to use?

Yes! VideoClipper is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium features. All functionality is available to everyone at no cost.

Do I need to install anything to use VideoClipper?

No. VideoClipper works entirely in your browser without any downloads or extensions.

Do I need to log in to use VideoClipper?

No login or sign-up is needed. It's completely free and anonymous to use.

Is VideoClipper safe to use?

Yes, it's secure and doesn't ask for login credentials, installations, or access to your personal data.

Does VideoClipper store my video clips?

No, VideoClipper does not permanently store any videos. Your clip is temporarily stored for a few minutes just to allow you to download it, and then it is automatically deleted from our servers.

Can I download the full video?

Yes, you can download the full video, but VideoClipper is mainly designed to let you select and download a specific part or clip of the video. This helps you save only the portion you need without downloading the entire file.

Is there a limit on the clip duration I can download?

There is no strict limit on the duration of the clip you select, but longer clips will take more time to process.

What video quality is supported?

You can download clips in resolutions up to 1080p, depending on the source video quality.

What websites are supported?

VideoClipper supports over 1,000 video platforms through yt-dlp. Popular supported sites include YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter (X), Vimeo, Twitch, Reddit, Coursera, LinkedIn, and Udemy. You can check out the full list of major supported sites on our Supported Sites page.

Can I download private videos?

It depends. VideoClipper uses yt-dlp and ffmpeg, so if they can't access a private video, neither can VideoClipper.

Does VideoClipper work on mobile devices?

Yes, the tool is fully optimized for mobile browsers as well as desktop.

Is there a usage limit?

Yes, you can download up to 3 clips every 30 minutes to keep the service stable for everyone.

What if my clip fails to process?

If your clip fails to process, it could be due to platform restrictions, video availability, or technical issues. Try again with a different time range or contact us for support.

Is it legal to use VideoClipper?

VideoClipper is a tool for creating clips from publicly available content. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to use and modify any content they process. Please respect copyright laws and platform terms of service.
