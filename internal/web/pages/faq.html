Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Hosted Clipper

Getting Started

What is Hosted Clipper?

Hosted Clipper is a web service that allows you to save and organize content from various websites. It helps you clip articles, videos, and other web content to view later or organize into collections.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of the page. You can sign up using your email address or through one of our social login options.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Hosted Clipper is available as a progressive web app (PWA) that works on both iOS and Android devices. You can add it to your home screen for quick access.

Using Hosted Clipper

How do I clip content from a website?

You can clip content in several ways: use our browser extension, share the URL directly from your mobile device, or manually paste the URL into the "Add New Clip" section of your dashboard.

Can I organize my clips into folders?

Yes! You can create custom collections and organize your clips into different folders. You can also add tags to make searching and filtering easier.

Is there a limit to how much I can save?

Free accounts have a limit of 100 clips. Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited clips and additional features like full-text search and offline access.

Privacy & Security

Is my data private?

Yes, your clips are private by default. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, and we don't sell or share your information with third parties.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export all your clips at any time. Go to your account settings and click on "Export Data" to download your information in a standard format.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any questions or issues you might have.
