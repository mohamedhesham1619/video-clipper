How to Cut Your Video Instantly

Step 1 Paste Your Video URL

Copy the URL of the video you want to cut from supported platforms like YouTube, Facebook or Reddit, and paste it into the "Video URL" input field.

Step 2 Set Your Desired Start and End Times

Select the desired start and end times in the HH:MM:SS format (e.g., 00:00:15 for 15 seconds, 00:01:30 for one minute and thirty seconds). The interface will provide live feedback on the selected duration, helping you refine your clip.

Step 3 Select Video Quality

Select the desired video quality from the dropdown menu. VideoClipper offers a range of options, including 360p, 480p, 720p, 1080p, and 2K, allowing you to balance between quality and file size.

Step 4 Download Your Perfect Clip

Click the "Download" button to start the download process. VideoClipper will begin processing your clip and provide a real-time progress tracking. The download will start automatically after the process is complete, and you can save the file to your device.
