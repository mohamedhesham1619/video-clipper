Get Started with VideoClipper for YouTube

Trimming a YouTube video doesn't have to be complicated. With VideoClipper, you can go from link to finished clip in seconds. This guide will walk you through copying a YouTube link, loading it into our tool, adjusting your timestamps, and saving your video in crisp HD or 2K.

How to Cut Your YouTube Video Instantly

Step 1 Get the YouTube Video Link

Open the YouTube video you want to cut. You can copy the link from the browser's address bar or click the Share button under the video to get a clean URL.

Step 2 Paste the YouTube Link into VideoClipper

Go to VideoClipper and paste the YouTube link into the Video URL field.

Step 3 Set Start and End Times

Select the desired start and end times in the HH:MM:SS format (e.g., 00:00:15 for 15 seconds, 00:01:30 for one minute and thirty seconds). The interface will provide live feedback on the selected duration, helping you refine your clip.

Step 4 Choose Video Quality

Select from YouTube's available resolutions — 360p for smaller file sizes, 720p or 1080p for HD, and up to 1440p (2K) for maximum clarity.

Step 5 Download Your Clip

Click the "Download" button to start the download process. VideoClipper will begin processing your clip and provide a real-time progress tracking. The download will start automatically after the process is complete, and you can save the file to your device.
